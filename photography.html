Street Art - Man United wall

Manchesters well known across the world football team Manchester United is displayed by a sticker stuck on a wall of graffiti, i liked this image because it's complementary to Manchester and the community. The use of bright colours also give of a young modern vibe to this image.

Street Art - Leaf

This is a image taken in the Northern Quarter of a leaf i used the leaf in focus with the street/ graffiti art in thebackground with the well known artist Liam Gallagher in plain sight. I wanted this image to scream Manc and make street art come from a different angle and even though its not in focus its still a big part of Manchester's community .

Street Art - Black and white girl

This is street art taken in the Northern Quarter, Manchester, this image captures a lady and from the art itself she looks upset the black and white was used to create a dull and dark theme and make people who view this image feel a certain way towards the image creating moods such as sadness.
